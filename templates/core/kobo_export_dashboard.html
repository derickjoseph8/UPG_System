How to Use with KoBoToolbox
Step 1: Download CSV Files

Click the download buttons above to get the CSV files you need.

Step 2: Upload to KoBoToolbox
  1. Open your form in KoBoToolbox
  2. Go to Settings > Media Files
  3. Upload the CSV files
Step 3: Use pulldata() in Your Form

In your XLSForm, use the pulldata() function to validate data:

Example XLSForm Formulas:

Validate Household ID:

pulldata('households', 'hh_id', 'hh_id', ${household_id})

Get Household Name:

pulldata('households', 'hh_name', 'hh_id', ${household_id})

Check Eligibility:

pulldata('households', 'is_eligible', 'hh_id', ${household_id}) = '1'
